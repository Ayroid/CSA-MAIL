Greetings from UPES CSA!

We are delighted to invite your esteemed organization to collaborate with us for the upcoming UPES CSA Hackathon 3.0, the third edition of our flagship event focused on digital security and urban technology in India.

UPES CSA Hackathon 3.0 is more than just a competition; it is a platform where creativity and innovation thrive, turning vision into reality. We believe that by bringing together diverse perspectives and experiences, we can create a more inclusive and innovative hackathon culture.
